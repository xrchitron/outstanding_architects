Metropolitan Architecture (OMA)

Dutch architectural firm Office for Metropolitan Architecture (OMA) and Hamburg-based Gerkan, Marg and Partners (GMP) were recently announced as the winners of the Chengdu Future Science and Technology City Launch Area Masterplan and Architecture Design Competition.

MVRDV

MVRDV is a Dutch architecture firm founded in 1993 by Winy Maas, Jacob van Rijs and Nathalie de Vries. The practice is headquartered in Rotterdam, with offices in Shanghai and Paris, and is made up of over 150 architects, designers and urbanists.
